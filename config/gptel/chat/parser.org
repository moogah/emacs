* Introduction

Scaffolded stub for =gptel-chat-parser= — the buffer walker that
converts a chat-mode buffer into a structured turn list and message
list consumed by =gptel-request=.

See =openspec/changes/gptel-chat-mode/architecture.md= §Components
(subsection =gptel-chat-parser (parser)=) for the full contract:
outer-block recognition, nested tool-block handling, structural
validation, and un-escaping of =,#+end_...= sequences.
